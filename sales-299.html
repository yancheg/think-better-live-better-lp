The Experience

Life can be hectic; sometimes chaos surrounds us in every imaginable direction. But just because the world around us is in disarray, doesn’t mean the world within us has to be too.

That’s right, I’m saying there’s a way to stay sane in insane times. I’m saying you can get rid of all the insanity and negativity inside you created by others, the past, and uncontrollable events...

"Think Better, Live Better" is your solution.

This one-day conference is packed full of practical content and unforgettably inspiring lessons for living a more positive and productive life. But this is more than an event. It's a training experience that will give you proven tools to identify and transform the negative, self-limiting beliefs and behaviors that keep you stuck.

You won't leave "Think Better, Live Better" with a notebook full of ideas and nothing checked off your to-do list. You'll set into motion a realistic plan you can keep improving on for years to come.

We will guide you step-by-step through mental strength training exercises and refocus your mind on the powerful truths that make the fastest and most effective impact on your personal desires and goals.

This day will be made up of engaging keynote presentations, actionable workshops and interactive panels from some of the brightest minds in personal growth and productivity.

Schedule

THINK BETTER, LIVE BETTER will be an immersive and invaluable experience taking place over one action-packed day in beautiful Austin, Texas.

What better way to kick off your day than with a series of morning keynote conversations that take you deep into the "Think Better, Live Better" topics -- ones that will serve as powerful, actionable moments of awakening. Then it's off to intimate workshops to actually put these strategies to work, followed by an open, interactive panel discussion with your presenters.

Flying to Austin (from within USA)

Fly into AUS (Austin-Bergstrom International Airport) on any of the major US carriers: SouthWest, United, Delta or American Airlines. (11 miles to W)
Flying to Austin (from Asia)

You'll have to transit at a major west coast airport hub.

Our suggestions: LAX (Los Angeles) or SFO (San Francisco).

If flying from a smaller Asian city, we recommend transiting through Hong Kong, Singapore, Taipei or Tokyo-Narita.
Flying to Austin (from Europe)

You may have to transit at a major east coast airport hub.

Our suggestions: IAH (Houston), IAD (Washington-Dulles) or YYZ (Toronto).

You can also fly direct with British Airways or American Airlines from major European capitals.

General faqs

Question 1. If I have a question about the event, how can I get in touch with you?

Please send as an email at info@thinklivebetter.com.

Question 2. What does it cost?

Early bird tickets are $299 and once they're gone, the regular price is $399.

Question 3. What is included in my ticket?

  • A seat at the table (literally!) at Think Better, Live Better. Everyone will be getting their own allocated seat.
  • We'll also include lunch in the form of a catered lunchbox.
  • Free tea, coffee, water will be provided throughout the event.
  • Access to the Think Better, Live Better Afterparty on the evening of the event. You'll have to grab your own drinks though.
  • Some super-secret SWAG which you'll only get if you come to Austin!

Question 4. What is the schedule?

Please see The Schedule above.

Question 5. Do I have to stay at the same hotel?

Absolutely not!

We don't get anything out of you staying at the W Austin - we just want our attendees to get the best deal and have the best experience possible!

Question 6. Can I share my hotel room with someone?

Absolutely. All confirmed attendees will be given access to a private Facebook group/event where you can contact other attendees and organize shared rooms.

Question 7. Can I bring a friend?

Why yes you can. Just make sure that they pick up their own ticket as well.

Question 8. What if I bought a ticket and I can't come anymore?

Because all seating is allocated to individual attendees at this event, we have a strict 7-day refund policy.
